About Us

This project was started to encourage people to care for plants and share that passion with others. Here you can post pictures of your plant(s) and like pictures of other people's plants. Every month we will choose 4 posts to show on the website's home page.

We will choose the posts based on the condition of the plant, such as its health, location, age, etc. In this day and age, we think it's important for people to care for plants and raise awareness of their importance to us all. We hope you enjoy your stay here at Flora Boards.
